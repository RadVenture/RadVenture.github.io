About

Radventure Mission

It is Radventure's mission to help travellers maximize their time, pocketbooks, and smiles while experiencing adventures in the great outdoors.

The Radventure Story

After parting ways with her “personal adventure guide” (aka break-up with boyfriend), Andrea felt a large void in her ability to really get out and about to experience the outdoors as she had before. One day, as she was going for a run, she had an A-HA! moment that she was likely not alone in this feeling of frustration. What if there were others out there with the desire to see, experience and conquer but they just need a local friend to help show them the way? That was when Radventure was born. We’ve all been there. You’re in a rad new place, but aren’t quite sure where to start. You wish you had a local buddy to give you the inside scoop. You chat with ski techs, bike shop staff, and bartenders, but what you really want is local to show you the sick spots so you can make the most your time outside.

Our Team

Courtney Gould
Director of Product

Courtney grew up in Colorado spending her free time exploring the mountains hiking and skiing. Her background working outdoor retail during college paved the way to a further employment and understanding of the outdoor industry and a deep love for the outdoors. Currently living in Portland, Courtney is a MBA student at Portland State, where Radventure was born. Courtney is handling product development with a focus on technical deployment. Outside of time spent on Radventure, Courtney spends the rest of her time racing bikes and cooking from scratch.

Andrea Bouma
Director of Supply Chain Management

Andrea has always had a love for the outdoors which only grew after her move out west from Michigan 10 years ago. Activities that she is passionate about include: snowsports, hiking, running, river adventuring, and generally having a good time out in the sun. Andrea’s background is in systems development from her past life as a product development food scientist. After completing a MBA in strategy and entrepreneurship from Portland State University, Andrea teamed up with Courtney and Robyn (two of the raddest members of her cohort) to create Radventure. A nomad at heart, Andrea is stoked to hit the road and work 1:1 with future Radventure guides to create a robust network of people helping people maximize their time having fun in the great outdoors.

Robyn Gelfand
Director of Marketing

Ski patroller, marketer, and ex-ski bum. Robyn’s love for the outdoors took off during a month-long Outward Bound course in the Elk Mountains of Colorado. It was there that she resolved to move to the mountains after college, and also to find a way to enable more people to enjoy the outdoors. After graduating from the University of Virginia, Robyn spent time teaching skiing in Steamboat Springs, CO before getting into marketing for brands including Coors Light, Miller Lite, and adidas. Robyn is currently a member of Mt. Hood Ski Patrol and a MBA student at Portland State University, and is managing marketing for Radventure.
